Product suite

Two core systems, one integrated offering

We deliver the full lifecycle — design, installation, and value-added services — for the systems that move and monitor high-purity gases and chemicals inside our customers’ plants.

Core system 01

High-Purity Process System

The high-purity process system applies effective control of impurities across the production process. It is used in the core processes of integrated-circuit manufacturing in the pan-semiconductor industry, and is the largest single contributor to our revenue.

  • High-purity gas conveyor system — special gas cabinets, valve manifold boxes, monitoring software, gas pipes and valve fittings. Used in doping, photolithography, etching, and CVD film-formation.
  • High-purity chemical conveyor system — chemical pipes, valves, chemical sensors, and monitoring software to convey the chemicals used in cleaning, corrosion, and grinding processes.

Factory Management & Control System

Our FMCS provides instant, effective monitoring of customers’ manufacturing processes through a central control room. We prioritize the software layer — the source of efficiency, scalability, and recurring engagement.

  • Gas Monitoring System (GMS) — around-the-clock control of gas transport; sends a signal that can shut the gas valve in time to prevent incidents.
  • Chemical Monitoring System (CMS) — monitors the safety of the chemical distribution process across the facility.
Why it scales

Software-led, modular, and built to expand

Modular by design

Standardized PLC modules let clients expand monitoring without re-engineering — expanding margins as the installed base grows.

Fewer updates, fewer errors

Mature algorithms mean adding detectors or cabinets requires no re-download of the PLC program, reducing downtime.

Customer-extensible

Software simulation lets the system be tested and improved without interrupting live equipment; customers can add monitoring applications themselves.
