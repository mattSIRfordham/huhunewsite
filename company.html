Company overview

Engineering purity into the world’s fabs

Founded in 2015, HUHUTECH designs, integrates, and monitors the high-purity systems that keep advanced manufacturing running. Through our operating subsidiaries, we serve semiconductor, optoelectronic, LED and micro-electronics, telecom, and logistics customers across Asia, Europe, and North America.

A diversifying base

HUHU Japan completed 199 projects in FY2025 and now drives 44% of revenue, up from 33.9% in FY2023. New subsidiaries in Germany, the United States, and Singapore extend the model to customers building fabs outside China.

Software modularity

  • Mature PLC algorithms: adding detectors, gas cabinets, or valve-manifold boxes requires no re-download of the PLC program.
  • Fewer program updates means fewer errors and less customer downtime.
  • Software simulation lets us test and improve without interrupting live equipment.
