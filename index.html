The investment case

Why HUHUTECH

A profitable, cash-generative systems integrator embedded in semiconductor capital spending.

Mission-critical to chip fabs

High-purity gas and chemical delivery plus facility monitoring are essential infrastructure in every advanced fab.

Revenue +18% to $21.4M

FY2025 revenue grew 18.1% year over year with gross profit up 8.1%; the business generated $2.9M of operating cash flow.

Five-country platform

Operating subsidiaries in China, Japan, Germany, the U.S., and Singapore — Japan alone now contributes 44% of revenue.

Defensible technology base

32 effective patents, 28 software copyrights, and ISO-certified processes underpin a modular, software-led product suite.

Underlying profitability

FY2025’s GAAP loss is driven almost entirely by an $18.4M non-cash share-based compensation charge; excluding it, operations were profitable.

Scalable, software-led model

Standardized PLC modules let clients expand monitoring without re-engineering — expanding margins as the installed base grows.

What we do

Two core systems, one integrated offering

High-Purity Process System

High-Purity Process System (HPS)

Customized high-purity gas and chemical delivery systems that control impurities across the core processes of integrated-circuit manufacturing — doping, photolithography, etching, and CVD film-formation. Our largest revenue contributor.

Factory Management & Control

Factory Management & Control System (FMCS)

Software-led, real-time monitoring that consolidates a plant’s facility systems into a single control center — with gas and chemical monitoring modules that can shut a valve in time to prevent incidents.
