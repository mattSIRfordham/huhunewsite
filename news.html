Scaling across global semiconductor clusters
MAY 2026

HUHU Deutschland awarded €13.9M (~$15M) contract from a Dresden foundry

The award is the largest single contract in Company history and validates the European expansion strategy, establishing HUHU Deutschland as a system-integration partner in one of Europe’s key semiconductor clusters.

MAY 2026

Follow-on $936K U.S. contract from Arizona anchor customer

HUHU USA secured a follow-on order from its anchor customer in the Arizona semiconductor corridor, building on the initial engagement secured in late 2025.

APR 2026

FY2025 annual results filed on Form 20-F

Revenue rose 18.1% to $21.4M and operating cash flow was a positive $2.9M. The reported GAAP loss was driven almost entirely by an $18.4M non-cash share-based compensation charge; excluding it, operations were profitable.

JAN 2026

Hiroshima Project Office opened; qualified-supplier status at a leading local fab

HUHU Japan deepened its presence in the Kyushu–Hiroshima corridor, supporting the subsidiary that now contributes 44% of consolidated revenue.

NOV 2025

First U.S. order in the Arizona semiconductor corridor

HUHU USA secured an initial $3.0M order, marking the Company’s entry into the U.S. market and extending the high-purity systems model to fabs being built in North America.
